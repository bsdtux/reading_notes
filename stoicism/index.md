## Seek out Misfortune

By seeking out misforutne one can begin to accept that there are forces in this world that are outside our control. Misfortune can also teach us appreciation for what we have as well as bring awareness that we can learn during these hard times

We can easily bring out misforutne by doing the following;

* Taking cold showers
* sitting in silence

## Turn the Obstacle Upside Down

By turning obstacles upside down you are looking at trials as opportunities to grow. In life events are just that events. They are neither bad nor good its just how we preceive those obstacles. For example if you lose your job, maybe its a chance to look for another one, or maybe that push you needed to start your own business. Remember that you can't control most obstacles but what you can control are;

* your peception: how you interpret / react to events
* action: how you respond to the event
* will: following your true path / not what you want

We can put this into practice by doing the following;

* List your worst experiences and how you handled it. Then list the other possible actions I could have taken and what was the possible outcome

## This too Will Pass

By understanding that events will come and go we begin to understand that we are small and trivial in this world. We should strive to be inward focused so that we can be virtous and do the right thing when the time comes. We should learn to live in the present. The past has already happened and the future is something that we can't control.

* Personal Thought: It reminds me of the lesson from Kungfu Panda, Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery but today is a gift. That is why it is called the present.

There is a lot we can gain from this. By being present we are living in the now and being attuned to what is happening right in front of us. So how can we be more present

Practical ways to be present;

## Embrace Your Lack of Control

When you embrace your lack of control you being to realize that you never really had control. There are very few things that you actually have control over. Even your own thoughts you don't have control over. its important that you begin to understand what you can and can't control. By realizing this you can release yourself from the contstant worrying and sadness when things happen that are out of your control.

Practice this saying everyday: Somethings are in my control and others are not. Ask what would my best self do when I run into things I can't control.

## Work Without Lust for Results

when you work specifically for a result, especially one your heart is set on, you will often be disappointed. Lets say you worked hard so you can score 3 goals next soccer game. What if you get rained out? What if you get injured? What if you trip and fall on your attempt to get your 3rd goal? There are things outside your control that can keep you from getting your result. So if you should not focus on the result, then what should you focus on. The answer to that is focus on the effort not the outcome. By focusing on the effort you are essentially focused on bringing about your best / authentic self.
